Frequently Asked Questions



What plan do you recommend?

We recommend you start with the Free plan and try the service before deciding to upgrade to any of the more powerful plans.
Does Fedora charge a setup fee?

There are absolutely no set up fees of any kind - you can launch your school entirely for free.


Who pays for hosting and video bandwidth?

All content hosting (including video) is completely included in the Fedora monthly and transaction fees. And yes, there is no limit to your bandwidth usage.
Am I allowed to offer free courses with Fedora?

Absolutely - you can offer free courses and there are no transaction fees with free courses at all. If you don't make money, we don't make money.


How do transaction fees work?

Every time a user purchases anything on your school, we collect the Transaction Fee according to your plan - the greater your monthly plan, the lower your transaction fee. You'll never have to pay more than the sale price of the course though - so if you are on the Free plan and sell a course for a $1, we'll only take $1 and not $1.10.

Additionally, if you use our payment processing, we also charge a standard credit card and Paypal processing fee of 30 cents + 2.9%.
What happens if I want to cancel?

We'll be sad to see you go, but you can cancel at absolutely anytime - there are no minimum lock-ins with your Fedora school.

In fact, since you own all the data and the domain name, we allow you to download all your user and financial data, so you can build your own website or use another service if that is what you would prefer.


When do I get paid?

You get paid at the end of every month for the previous month - so at the end of March, you'd get paid for February. The reason we have it set up this way is end users generally have 30 days to request a refund.

The only exception to this is on our Unlimited plan where the money would go directly into your Stripe account and you'd receive daily bank deposits.
Can I use my own domain name?

In all plans Basic upwards, you can use any number of domain names you'd like to point to the same Fedora school.

Additionally, every plan comes with a completely free yourschool.usefedora.com domain name that you are also completely welcome to use.


Do I need a web host?

No, you do not - Fedora is fully hosted (even though it looks like you are hosting it!) so you don't need to worry about hosting.
Do I need a payment processor?

Fedora handles the payment processing for you so you can breathe easy. We accept credit and debit cards from around the world and Paypal - with Bitcoin processing coming very soon.


Can Fedora be used for enterprise applications like internal training?

Yes, absolutely - some of our biggest clients use Fedora in that way. Our enterprise plans have a host of powerful features like a custom SSL certificate, single sign-on integration and private servers, e-mail [email protected] for more details.
I have more questions - who can I contact?

Once you create an account, you can use the Support link in the bottom-left of the admin panel. Alternatively, you are always welcome to e-mail [email protected] if you have any questions prior to creating your school.
